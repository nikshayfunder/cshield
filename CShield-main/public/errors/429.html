CShield Edge Error code: 429

Too many requests

Your client sent too many requests in a short period. Protection systems temporarily throttled traffic.

What happened?

Rate limiting or surge detection was triggered. This helps keep the service available during high load and abusive bursts.

What can I do?

  • Wait a few seconds and retry
  • Avoid sending parallel or automated requests
  • Site owners can adjust thresholds
